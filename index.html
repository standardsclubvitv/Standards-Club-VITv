About BIS Standards

The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) is the national standards body of India, ensuring quality and safety across industries. BIS plays a crucial role in setting benchmarks for various products, services, and processes, promoting reliability and trust in the marketplace.

Through its certification programs, BIS helps in maintaining high-quality standards, ensuring consumer safety, and facilitating international trade.
