About Us

The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), the National Standards Body of India, is implementing the programme of Standard Clubs in schools and colleges across the country.

The Standards Club is established with the objective of sensitizing young members of society about the importance of standards in improving the quality of life.

Our mission is to transform young minds into knowledgeable ambassadors of quality and standards in everything around them.
